Company Introduction

About Us

The brand was established in 2012. Our main business encompasses the design and manufacture of automobiles, motorcycles, motorboats, ATVs, UTVs, as well as accessories and supplies related to RVs.

  • Design Excellence: Several outstanding designs from our design team have won relevant awards multiple times.
  • Manufacturing Capabilities: Our own motor vehicle manufacturing factory provides customers with high-quality customized motor vehicle services.
  • Certifications: World Manufacturing Plant qualification issued by the SAE Engineer Alliance (USA), and TS16949 management system under the ISO9001 certification.

Customized Business

Powersports Customization

1

Consultation

Communicating with the customer to understand their needs.

2

Design Phase

Discussing the design and repeatedly modifying it until the customer is satisfied with the final draft.

3

Production

Starting production based on the parameters of the drawings.

4

Craftsmanship

All accessories are self-made without using mass-produced parts. Although time-consuming, this guarantees a unique customized vehicle.

Track Specific Vehicle Customization

1. Basic Modification of Primary Circuit Car

  • Adjustable height separation handle
  • Exhaust modification
  • Adjustable height pedal
  • Chain & Tires upgrade
  • Racing car shell
  • Brake pipe upgrade
  • Gearbox tuning (number of gears, gear ratio, and power output)
  • Dedicated limiters and steering damping
  • Customization and calibration of shock absorbers

2. Intermediate Racing Car Modification

  • ECU upgrade
  • Internal engine component upgrade
  • Overall lightweighting of the vehicle body
